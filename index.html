About Me

I am a lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Farest International University, with a focus on neural networks, deep learning, and machine learning. My passion lies in exploring the transformative potential of artificial intelligence to address real-world challenges. As a professional educator, I have always been interested in creating a dynamic learning environment that encompasses critical thinking, practical experimentation, and cooperation. My goal is to inspire students to excel academically and help them achieve success in today's fast-evolving technology industry. Outside of teaching, I am actively engaged in research projects applying AI techniques to solve practical problems. My work bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation by exploring areas like predictive modeling, data analysis, and ethical AI. I also anticipate a great possibility for interdisciplinary cooperation on the implementation of AI to support various branches like healthcare, education, or financial services. Contributing with my accumulated experience to the development and further growth of this area, I will be mentoring future leaders within technology.
